Employ the reactingFoam solver to perform a 2-dimensional laminar counterflow flame simulation with methane (CH4) as fuel and air as oxidizer. The simulation utilizes the LTS (Local Time Stepping) approach, the GRI-Mech 3.0 chemical mechanism (GRI), and TDAC (Tabulation of Dynamic Adaptive Chemistry) for chemistry acceleration.

Initial and boundary conditions are as follows:
*   Fuel inlet (boundary 'fuel'): Velocity fixed at (0.1 0 0) m/s, temperature fixed at 293 K, CH4 mass fraction fixed at 1.0, O2, H2O, CO2, and default inert mass fractions fixed at 0.0.
*   Air inlet (boundary 'air'): Velocity fixed at (-0.1 0 0) m/s, temperature fixed at 293 K, O2 mass fraction fixed at 0.23, N2 mass fraction fixed at 0.77, CH4, H2O, CO2, and default inert mass fractions fixed at 0.0.
*   Outlet (boundary 'outlet'): Total pressure condition set to 1e5 Pa, inletOutlet conditions for velocity and species mass fractions (Yi) allowing outflow or reverting to internal field values for inflow.
*   Initial uniform internal field: Pressure 1e5 Pa, Temperature 2000 K, Velocity (0 0 0) m/s. CH4, O2, H2O, CO2, and default inert mass fraction fields are uniform 0.0; N2 mass fraction field is uniform 1.0.
*   Thermophysical properties: Perfect gas equation of state, JANAF thermodynamics, Sutherland transport model.

Discretization schemes defined in `fvSchemes` include:
*   Time derivative: `localEuler`
*   Gradient: `Gauss linear`
*   Divergence: Primarily `limitedLinear`/`limitedLinearV 1` for velocity (`phi,U`), species/enthalpy (`phi,Yi_h`), kinetic energy (`phi,K`), dissipation rate (`phi,epsilon`), pressure correction (`phid,p`), and stress term; `linear` for turbulence stress term; `none` default overridden.
*   Laplacian: `Gauss linear orthogonal`
*   Interpolation: `linear`
*   Surface normal gradient: `orthogonal`

Solution algorithms in `fvSolution`:
*   Pressure (p): Solved using PCG (Preconditioned Conjugate Gradient) with DIC (Diagonal Incomplete-Cholesky) preconditioner (tolerance 1e-6, relTol 0.1).
*   Velocity (U), enthalpy (h), species mass fractions (Yi): Solved using PBiCGStab (Preconditioned Bi-Conjugate Gradient Stabilized) with DILU (Diagonal Incomplete-LU) preconditioner (tolerance 1e-6, relTol 0.1).
*   Density (rho) solved diagonally.
*   PIMPLE (merged PISO-SIMPLE): Momentum prediction disabled (`momentumPredictor no`), outer correctors set to 1 (`nOuterCorrectors 1`), inner correctors set to 1 (`nCorrectors 1`), no non-orthogonal correction (`nNonOrthogonalCorrectors 0`), no equation relaxation (`relaxationFactors { equations { ".*" 1; } }`).
