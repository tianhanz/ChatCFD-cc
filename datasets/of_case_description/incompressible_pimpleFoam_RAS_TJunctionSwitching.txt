A turbulent incompressible flow simulation is required for a T-junction with switching behavior using OpenFOAM's pimpleFoam solver. The PISO-SIMPLE algorithm (PIMPLE) applies with 1 outer corrector and 2 corrector steps.

Divergence schemes apply limitedLinearV (limiter=1) for momentum flux and limitedLinear for turbulence transport. Gradient and Laplacian terms use Gauss linear and Gauss linear corrected schemes respectively.

Turbulence at inlet is defined by 5% intensity (k) and mixing length (0.01m) dissipation (epsilon). Wall functions handle turbulence near boundaries.
