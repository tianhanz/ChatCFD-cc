This simulation involves a 2-dimensional vortex shedding flow past a cylinder using the large eddy simulation (LES) approach. The pimpleFoam solver is employed for incompressible transient flow with the SpalartAllmarasDDES turbulence model. The fluid is Newtonian with a constant kinematic viscosity of 1e-05 m²/s.

Initial and boundary conditions are defined as follows: the internal velocity field is uniformly initialized to (0.025 0 0) m/s. At the inlet, velocity is fixed at this uniform value; at the cylinder surface, a no-slip condition (fixed value of (0 0 0)) is applied, while the top and bottom boundaries use a slip condition. The outlet boundary employs an inletOutlet condition for velocity and modified pressure (p). Pressure starts uniform at 0 internally, with zeroGradient conditions at the inlet, cylinder, top, and bottom. The turbulent viscosity (nuTilda) initializes uniformly to 1e-05 m²/s, with fixedValue at the inlet and zeroGradient at the cylinder.

Discretization settings use a backward scheme for time derivatives. Gradients use Gauss linear. Divergence schemes include Gauss LUST grad(U) for momentum flux to ensure stability in LES, and limitedLinear 1 for nuTilda. Laplacian terms apply Gauss linear corrected.

Solution algorithms under PIMPLE control comprise 5 outer correctors and 2 pressure correctors per time step, with no non-orthogonal correctors. Solver settings specify GAMG with DICGaussSeidel smoother for pressure (relTol 0.05), and PBiCGStab with DILU preconditioner for velocity and nuTilda (relTol 0.1). Relaxation factors of 0.8 are applied to nuTilda, U, and p fields before final convergence.
