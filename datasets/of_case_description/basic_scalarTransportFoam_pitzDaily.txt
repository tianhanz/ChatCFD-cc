Using scalarTransportFoam to perform a 2-dimensional simulation of passive scalar transport in a flow domain configured for the pitzDaily case. The velocity field is predefined with an inlet fixed at 10 m/s in the x-direction, no-slip conditions on the upper and lower walls, and a zero-gradient pressure outlet. The scalar field T, initialized uniformly to zero, is introduced at the inlet with a value that transitions from a very small magnitude to 1.0 after a brief initial period. The discretization employs the Euler scheme for time derivatives, linear upwind for the convective term of T, and linear corrected for the diffusive laplacian term. The scalar equation is solved using the PBiCGStab method with DILU preconditioner and a tolerance of 1e-6.
