Simulate transient incompressible flow through a domain containing a porous obstruction using the pisoFoam solver. The simulation assumes laminar flow of a Newtonian fluid with kinematic viscosity nu = 0.005 m²/s. A Darcy-Forchheimer explicit porosity source model is applied within the cell zone named 'porousBlockage', using coefficient D = 1000 to induce significant flow blockage and wake shedding behavior. The fluid initially has uniform velocity (1, 0, 0) m/s and uniform relative pressure 0 Pa. Boundary conditions include fixedValue velocity at inlet, pressureInletOutletVelocity at outlet, and zeroGradient pressure at inlet.  

For discretization, the Euler scheme is used for time derivatives. Gradients employ Gauss linear interpolation. The divergence term for the velocity flux (phi,U) utilizes the Gauss LUST grad(U) scheme with a limiter. Laplace terms apply Gauss linear uncorrected discretization.  

Solution settings use the PISO algorithm with 2 correction steps and no non-orthogonal correctors. Pressure is solved with the GAMG linear solver, using Gauss-Seidel smoothing, a relative tolerance of 0.1, and absolute tolerance 1e-06. Velocity components employ the smoothSolver with Gauss-Seidel smoother and an absolute tolerance of 1e-05.
