Simulating laminar two-phase flow through a porous medium using the interIsoFoam solver for the discInConstantPorousFlow case. Flow occurs within a porous domain where porosity effects are activated. Initial conditions include a uniform velocity field set to (1, 0, 0.5) m/s throughout the domain, zero initial modified pressure (p_rgh), and zero initial water volume fraction (alpha.water).
