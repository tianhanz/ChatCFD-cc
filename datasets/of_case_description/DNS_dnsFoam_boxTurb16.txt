Simulating homogeneous isotropic turbulence using the dnsFoam solver without explicit turbulence modeling. Initialization employs synthetic turbulence with boxTurbDict settings of Ea=10 and k0=5, supplemented by turbulenceProperties constants including UOsigma=0.090295 and UOKupper=10. The kinematic viscosity (nu) is 0.025 from transportProperties. All boundaries are cyclic across six patches.

The discretization uses Euler time stepping, Gauss linear gradients, and a higher-order Gauss cubic divergence scheme specifically for the velocity flux (div(phi,U)). Laplacian terms use Gauss linear corrected, while surface normal gradients use the corrected scheme. Pressure is solved with PCG using DIC preconditioner and tight tolerance (1e-06). Velocity utilizes the smoothSolver with symmetric Gauss-Seidel smoothing and a tolerance of 1e-05. The PISO algorithm is configured for 2 pressure correctors and no non-orthogonal correctors.
