Simulate a compressible turbulent annular thermal mixer using the rhoPimpleFoam solver with the k-epsilon turbulence model. PIMPLE algorithm applies 3 outer correctors and 1 momentum corrector without non-orthogonal correction. Initial fields set pressure uniformly to 1e5 Pa, velocity to zero, temperature to 293 K, and turbulence quantities to inner inlet values.
