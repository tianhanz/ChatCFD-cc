Conduct a 2D steady-state incompressible turbulent flow simulation around a fixed wing using the simpleFoam solver with the kOmegaSST turbulence model. The fluid is Newtonian with kinematic viscosity ν = 1e-05 m²/s. Boundary conditions include: fixed velocity, turbulent kinetic energy (k), and specific dissipation rate (ω) at the inlet; fixed pressure with inletOutlet conditions for velocity and turbulence variables at the outlet; no-slip velocity and wall functions (kqRWallFunction, omegaWallFunction) on the wing surface; and symmetry/empty conditions on front/back/top/bottom boundaries. Initial fields are uniform for pressure, velocity, k, and ω.

Discretization employs: steady-state time derivatives, linear Gauss gradient schemes, bounded linearUpwind divergence for momentum, upwind divergence for turbulence convection, linear corrected laplacian schemes, and corrected face-normal gradients. The SIMPLE algorithm solves momentum and pressure without non-orthogonal corrections. Pressure uses GAMG solving with 1e-7 tolerance and 0.3 relaxation, while velocity and turbulence variables use smoothSolver with Gauss-Seidel smoothing, 1e-8 tolerance, and 0.7 relaxation.
