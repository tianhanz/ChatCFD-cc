This simulation models multiphase flow within a mixing vessel using the interFoam solver. The setup involves water and air phases interacting under the influence of gravity (g = (0, 0, -9.81 m/s²)). A rotating stirrer is implemented via solid-body motion around the z-axis at 5 rad/s, utilizing cyclic Arbitrary Mesh Interface (AMI) boundaries. The turbulence is modeled with the standard k-epsilon formulation. Key settings include:

*   **Phases:** Water (density ρ = 300 kg/m³, kinematic viscosity ν = 1e-6 m²/s) and Air (density ρ = 1027 kg/m³, kinematic viscosity ν = 1e-6 m²/s), both Newtonian fluids. Surface tension coefficient σ = 0.07 N/m.
*   **Initial Conditions:**
    *   Volume fraction `alpha.water` = 0 (no water present initially throughout most of the domain, except `gasInlet` where `alpha.water` = 1).
    *   Velocity `U` = (0, 0, 0) m/s everywhere.
    *   Turbulent kinetic energy `k` = 0.01 m²/s², dissipation rate `epsilon` = 0.007 m²/s³.
    *   Turbulent viscosity `nut` = 0 m²/s.
    *   Modified pressure `p_rgh` = 1.25e5 Pa.
*   **Key Solver & Scheme Settings:**
    *   `alpha.water` uses 1 correction and 2 sub-cycles with `cAlpha = 1`.
    *   Pressure `p_rgh` solved with GAMG (tolerance 1e-6, relTol 0.02, GaussSeidel smoother).
    *   Velocity `U`, `k`, `epsilon` solved with smoothSolver (symGaussSeidel smoother, tolerance 1e-6, no relTol, 1 sweep).
    *   Time derivative: Euler.
    *   Gradient schemes: Limited linear for `U`, Gauss linear otherwise.
    *   Convection schemes: `vanLeer` for `alpha.water`, `linearUpwind grad(U)` for `rhoPhi,U`, `upwind` for turbulence quantities.
    *   Laplacian/gradient schemes: Limited linear corrected (0.33 coef).
    *   PIMPLE loop: 2 outer correctors, 1 velocity corrector, momentum prediction `yes`, correctPhi `yes`, no non-orthogonal correction.
    *   Relaxation factors = 1 (no relaxation).
*   **Key Boundary Conditions:**
    *   **`gasInlet`:** Fixed value for `U` = (0, 0, 0.5 m/s), `alpha.water` = 1 (pure air), turbulence quantities set.
    *   **Outlet:** `p_rgh` fixed value, `U` and turbulence quantities use `inletOutlet`.
    *   **Rotating Walls (`shaftRotating`, `stirrer`, `shaft`):** `movingWallVelocity` or `rotatingWallVelocity` with specified rotation parameters.
    *   **AMI Boundaries (`AMI.*`):** `cyclicAMI` type.
    *   Other walls use `fixedValue` or `zeroGradient` for `alpha`; `fixedFluxPressure`, `kqRWallFunction`, `epsilonWallFunction`, `nutkWallFunction` for pressure and turbulence.
