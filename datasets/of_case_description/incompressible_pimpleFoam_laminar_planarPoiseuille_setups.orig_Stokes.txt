Conduct a laminar planar Poiseuille flow simulation using the pimpleFoam solver for incompressible flow with a Stokes viscoelastic fluid model under the Stokes framework. The fluid is initially stationary with zero initial pressure, velocity, and viscoelastic stress fields. Boundary conditions enforce no-slip walls with fixed zero velocity, while pressure and stress fields use zero-gradient conditions. Key discretization employs backward time integration, Gauss linear gradient calculation, and divergence schemes including linearUpwind for velocity transport and vanAlbada-limited convection for stress components. Solver settings utilize GAMG with DIC smoothing for pressure at 1e-6 tolerance, while velocity and stress employ Gauss-Seidel smoothing at identical tolerance levels. PIMPLE algorithm operates without momentum prediction, applying 15 outer and 3 inner corrections per step without relaxation. A constant body force of (5,0,0) m/s² activates immediately throughout the domain to establish the pressure-driven flow profile. Model parameters configure Stokes kinematic viscosity (nuM) at 1 m²/s and relaxation time (lambda) at 5 s to characterize the fluid's viscoelastic response.
