Using icoFoam to perform a 3D lid-driven cavity simulation focusing on field mapping validation. The cavity features incompressible laminar flow with kinematic viscosity nu = 0.01 m²/s. The top wall (movingWall) slides tangentially at 1 m/s in the x-direction while other walls remain stationary. Initial conditions set velocity uniformly to zero and pressure uniformly to zero, with empty boundary conditions on front/back planes.

Discretization uses Euler scheme for time derivatives, Gauss linear for gradients and divergence terms (linear interpolation), and Gauss linear orthogonal for Laplacians. The PISO algorithm handles pressure-velocity coupling with 2 corrector steps and zero non-orthogonal correctors. Pressure solving employs PCG with DIC preconditioner (tolerance 1e-6, relTol 0.05), while velocity uses smoothSolver with symGaussSeidel smoother (tolerance 1e-5, relTol 0). Pressure reference cell is fixed at 0 Pa.
